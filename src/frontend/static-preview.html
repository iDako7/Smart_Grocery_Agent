Korean BBQ Pork Belly
Gochujang · grill · Medium · 30 min
✕ ▾
slice pork belly 3-4mm thick marinade (30 min): 2 tbsp gochujang · 1 tbsp soy sauce 1 tbsp sesame oil · 2 cloves garlic, minced grill high heat, 2-3 min/side char marks = done serve with rice, lettuce wraps
Grilled Corn & Cucumber Salad
Side · grill · Quick · 15 min
✕ ▾
grill corn 10-12 min, turning often let cool, slice kernels off cob cucumber: slice thin, salt 5 min, pat dry dressing: 2 tbsp rice vinegar · 1 tbsp sesame oil 1 tsp sugar · pinch chili flake toss all, serve cold
Classic Smash Burgers
Cast iron · Quick · 15 min
✕ ▾
heat cast iron until smoking loosely ball 80g patty, place on pan smash flat immediately — hold 10 sec season salt + pepper cook 90 sec, flip once, add cheese toast buns cut-side down, 30 sec

How PCV analysis works

We check your ingredients across three categories to find structural gaps in your meal plan:

Protein — meats, fish, tofu, beans
Carb — rice, bread, noodles, potatoes
Veggie — fresh produce, greens, roots

Sauce is tracked internally but shown only in the grocery list. This helps us suggest balanced meals without overthinking.
