Korean BBQ pork belly

Gochujang · grill · Medium · spicy, umami

✕
Slice pork belly 3-4mm thick Marinade (30 min): 2 tbsp gochujang 1 tbsp soy sauce 1 tbsp sesame oil 2 cloves garlic, minced Grill high heat, 2-3 min per side Char marks = done Serve with rice, lettuce wraps
Grilled corn + cucumber salad

Classic BBQ side · Quick · smoky, fresh

✕
Corn: grill 10 min, turning often Brush with butter + chili flakes Cucumber salad: Smash cucumbers, tear into chunks 2 tbsp rice vinegar 1 tbsp sesame oil Pinch sugar, salt Top with sesame seeds
Classic smash burgers

With cheese · Quick · savory, rich

✕
Form loose balls ~2 oz each Cast iron or flat griddle, max heat Smash flat with spatula, season 2 min until edges crisp Flip, add cheese, 1 min Bun: toast cut-side on griddle Stack: bun, patty, lettuce, sauce

Salt & pepper chicken wings

Chinese · Long · serves 4 · salty, numbing, spicy

✕
air fryer or oven Kenji / The Wok
Edit
Baking powder : starch : salt = 1 : 1 : 0.5 (~1 tsp per 1 lb of wings) Toss wings in mixture, shake off excess Air-dry mode (pick one): Convection 150-170°F, 30-60 min Or refrigerator overnight, uncovered Bake 450°F, ~15 min per side Until skin is crispy and golden Toss with: Fried garlic, sliced chili, salt White pepper to taste
